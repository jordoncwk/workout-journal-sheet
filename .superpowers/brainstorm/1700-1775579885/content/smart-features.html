Smart Features for Mandarin Learning

Pick any that interest you — select multiple

1

Spaced Repetition (SRS)

After each flashcard, mark it Easy / Hard / Forgot. The app schedules the next review automatically — words you struggle with come back sooner. Same system used by Anki.

2

Auto Tone Marks on Pinyin

Type pinyin with numbers (e.g. ni3 hao3) and the app converts to proper tone marks (nǐ hǎo) automatically. No fiddling with special characters.

3

Audio Pronunciation

Tap a speaker icon to hear the Mandarin pronunciation of any entry using text-to-speech. Great for checking your tones without a teacher.

4

HSK Level Badge

Automatically tag each entry with its HSK level (1–6). Lets you see at a glance how advanced your vocabulary is and filter by level.

5

Streak & Progress Stats

Daily learning streak, words added this week, review accuracy over time. Simple dashboard to keep you motivated.

6

Full-Text Search

Instantly search across characters, pinyin, English, or your notes. Find that word you half-remember from last week.

7

Character Breakdown

For each character, show the radical components and their individual meanings. Helps you remember characters by understanding their parts (e.g. 明 = 日 sun + 月 moon = bright).

8

Quick Add from Clipboard

Copy a Chinese sentence from anywhere (WeChat, Safari, etc.) and paste it into the app to pre-fill an entry. Saves typing.

9

Export to Anki / CSV

Export your journal entries as Anki flashcard decks or CSV. Use your own data in other tools without being locked in.

My top picks for a beginner/intermediate learner: 1 (SRS), 2 (Tone Marks), 3 (Audio), 5 (Streak), 6 (Search)
